About EduBridge
EduBridge is a WhatsApp-first adaptive learning platform built for Nigerian families. We deliver one personalized Math, English, or Science question a day directly to parents’ WhatsApp, adjusting to each child’s level with hints and encouragement. Weekly progress updates keep parents in the loop, while teachers get a simple dashboard to track learning and send announcements in English, Pidgin, Yoruba, Hausa, and Igbo. No smartphone, no expensive data, no barriers — just WhatsApp. EduBridge bridges the gap between home and school to close the learning gap and give every child a fair chance to succeed.
